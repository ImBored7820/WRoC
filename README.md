My advanced programming topics final project

STAGE 1: Map + Player character creation and 
customization and basic player behaviors 

STAGE 2: Top bar with minimap, opponnent info, 
player info, as well as an inventory and player 
status page with their class and stats

STAGE 3: Definitions for the classes Language, STEM,
History, & Sports, as well as stat definitions for
mind body soul as well as level system

STAGE 4: Cafeteria, trade, currency, items, weapons, 
loot in hallways and reward system 

STAGE 5: Basic Mobs, "Zombies", "Spiders", hall monitors

So these are the steps I envision and most likely I wont get as far but this is like what im imagining, I’ll split these tasks into Stages and set a goal stage, below are what the user is going to be able to do

Customize what character looks like, choose a name
Tutorial led by Mr. Millhollen, where they learn controls, learn about stats and classes as well as levels and the currency, ending off with a “test fight” against Mr. Millhollen which the user will lose no matter what
Choose a subject and begin at level 0 with starting gear depending on what subject you chose.
So for level 0 subjects you’ll have the really basic ones Language, STEM, History, & Sports, Languages will be enchanting/rune spell related dependent on which language you choose, English, Spanish, German etc, History will be wizard spells, like element related, Stem is going to have a lot, it’s going to be gadget makers, alchemists and like engineering as well as business (merchants) and more finally Sports will be more so physical attributions like becoming faster, stronger, using “weapons” like pencil swords, eraser bows etc.
At level 10 you can choose a specific class that has no pre requisites, like english 9, algebra 1, intro to programming etc at 20 you can upgrade your subject/level 10 class to its next one (APCSA -> Advanced Programming topics) this will be called a specialization
To progress level you need to defeat mobs/bosses and gain xp, xp will scale per level
Stats - So this will have a stat system, mine is going to be unique in regards to the fact that your subject decides the stats you can change/ how each stat effects you, so the stats will be Mind, Body, & Soul
The Language & History subject and their classes will be dependent on Mind & Soul
The STEM subjects will be dependent on Mind & Body
Sports will be dependent on Soul & Body
Stats will be displayed in this format: M-B-S
Choosing Language or History gives you a starting build of 2-1-3
Choosing stem gives you 3-2-1
Choosing sports gives you 1-3-2
Each level you gain 1 stat point in each of your dependencies and 1 which you can put anywhere
So if I am Language 0 and gain a level my stats will go from 2-1-3 to 3-1-4 and I will have a spare stat which I can put anywhere
Spawn into cafeteria, you can shop, trade and chill there it’s a safe zone
Explore school 3 levels, each level has a roaming boss (Vice-Principal), and hall monitors (Regular mobs)
Defeating hall monitors & roaming bosses sends them to the principal who tells them to like take paid leave or something pg
